OUR STORY

Building trust through transparency

CarTale was born from a simple belief: purchasing a reconditioned vehicle should feel as refined as the vehicle itself. Founded by automotive enthusiasts who were frustrated by the opacity of the pre-owned market, we set out to create a more transparent alternative.

Every vehicle in our collection is hand-selected and rigorously inspected. We insist on documentation clarity, fair market pricing, and secure offline transaction closure to ensure complete confidence in every purchase.

2018

Founded with a mission to bring structure and transparency to Nepal’s reconditioned vehicle market. Began with a curated collection of carefully inspected vehicles.

2020

Expanded operations and formalized a multi-point inspection standard. Introduced structured documentation review and improved vehicle evaluation procedures.

2022

Strengthened transparency initiatives and improved client verification processes. Built long-term customer relationships based on reliability and consistency.

2024

Recognized locally for disciplined processes and customer-first operations. Continued expansion while maintaining inspection integrity and pricing fairness.
